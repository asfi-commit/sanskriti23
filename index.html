A Few Words About AFSU sanskriti 2023

Your AFSU

The Arts Faculty Students Union (AFSU) is the elected students union of the Arts Faculty of Jadavpur University. A union that strives to maintain transparency, it is a union of the students, by the students and for the students. Time and again, AFSU has proven to be a space for raising voice against any unjust steps that may undermine the students' interest. In a historical note, AFSU, as a space of unionized students, had been able to resist a lot of anti-students stances by the University authority, and maintain the democratic space in the campus. Aside from that, AFSU also regularly provides economic support to students in need, from the faculty. The Arts Faculty Students Union, also organizes Freshers for the Arts Faculty, various sports activities, as well as fests, which includes AFSU Sanskriti.

Your SANSKRITI

Even though the year comes to an end, and the pressure of semester examinations hovers over the heads of many, AFSU Sanskriti manages to bring about the only glimmer of hope of absolute delight and entertainment in the new year for all the students of Jadavpur University -- a fest that the entire state, if not the country, looks forward to. Being one of the most prominent fests in Jadavpur University, AFSU Sanskriti is not merely a fest, but a space to celebrate and embrace the institution's spirit of resistance. "Sanskriti", meaning culture, lives up to it's true meaning and becomes a experience of a lifetime. AFSU Sanskriti has been a platform and a safe space for many students and guest artists, and not just for the commercially successful artists, but ones who aren't mainstream yet have made and strive to make good music. Sanskriti has proven to be a stage for baul, and various folk artists, aside from previous popular sensations such as Indian Ocean, Shilajit, Saptak Sanai Das, Rupankar, and Chandrabindoo. With 20+ events, 10+ sports activities, more than 3.5k student involvement, and about 50+ social media outreach, over a course of a week, AFSU Sanskriti always manages to kickstart the new year with culture and unity among all.

Like every year AFSU Sanskriti will host a variety of events and ensure the participation and preservation of young and willing hearts as celebratory moments in history. AFSU Sanskriti has been a beautiful endeavour of creativity. Including the varied spectrum of cultural performances it engages innumerable students encouraging them to contribute their own shares of unique creativity. The varied representations of Indian culture through different art forms is the purpose of Sanskriti. It not only encourages talent but also gathers people together and weaves them in a network of joy through art. The contemporary education system is more about individualistic segregation rather than humanitarian values. We have been demoted from the collective identity of students to programmed robots of 21st century. Yet, being an event of this contemporary world, Sanskriti is, as previously said , an endeavour towards holistic learning.
