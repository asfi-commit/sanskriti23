Your AFSU

The Arts Faculty Students Union (AFSU) is the elected students union of the Arts Faculty of Jadavpur University.

A union that strives to maintain transparency, it is a union of the students, by the students and for the students.

Time and again, AFSU has proven to be a space for raising voice against any unjust steps that may undermine the students' interest.

In a historical note, AFSU, as a space of unionised students, had been able to resist a lot of anti-students stances by the University authority, and maintain the democratic space in the campus. Aside from that, AFSU also

regularly strives to lend economic support by crowdfunding to people in need, from the faculty, or people who help the students in many ways in the University campus. The Arts Faculty Students Union, also organises Freshers for the Arts Department, various sports activities, as well as fests, which includes AFSU Sanskriti.

Your SANSKRITI

Like every year AFSU Sanskriti will host a variety of events and ensure the participation and preservation of young and willing hearts as celebratory moments in history..

AFSU Sanskriti has been a beautiful endeavour of creativity. Including the varied spectrum of cultural performances it engages innumerable students encouraging them to contribute their own shares of unique creativity.

The varied representations of Indian culture through different art forms is the purpose of Sanskriti. It not only encourages talent but also gathers people together and weaves them in a network of joy through art.

The contemporary education system is more about individualistic segregation rather than humanitarian values. We have been demoted from the collective identity of students to programmed robots of 21st century. Yet, being an event of this contemporary world, Sanskriti is, as previously said , an endeavour towards holistic learning
